Frequently Asked Questions

Therapy with MindAid


MindAid offers a full spectrum of digital mental health supports, from face-to-face live therapy, therapist-guided programs, messaging counselling, and free content.
Everyone has different needs. So, there's no one solution that fits everyone. You choose what works for you.

We provide two different services: Asynchronous messaging and Live Sessions.
Please visit our homepage for a detailed comparison for each of our services.

From the very start, MindAid's mission has been to break down barriers to mental health support and price was one of the first we tackled.
We have free volunteer services for you. Also you can have free trials of sessions.
